This tactic focuses on maintaining control of the ball, often referred to as Tiki-Taka. It frustrates opponents by limiting their time on the ball and creating openings through patient passing.

Key Principles:

  • **Patient Passing:** Short, quick passes to maintain rhythm.
  • **Triangles:** Players constantly create passing triangles to ensure options.
  • **Counter-Pressing:** Winning the ball back immediately after it's lost.

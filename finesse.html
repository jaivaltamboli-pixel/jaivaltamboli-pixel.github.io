A finesse shot is about precision and curve, not raw power. Using the inside of the foot, the attacker curls the ball around defenders and into the corner beyond the keeper.

It focuses on placement, timing, body shape, and vision to bend the ball accurately.

Key Mechanics

  • Inside-foot curving technique
  • Far-post or top-corner targets
  • Used when there's space to curl around defenders
  • Perfect for close control and tight angles

The Finesse Shot involves placing the ball with curve and precision, usually aiming for the far post. It prioritizes accuracy and deception over raw power, making it highly effective outside the box.

Key Principles:

  • **Body Shape:** Leaning slightly away from the ball at the point of contact.
  • **Inside Foot:** Using the inside of the foot for maximum curve.
  • **Targeting:** Aiming far post to beat the goalkeeper's dive.

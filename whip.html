The Whip Cross is a fast, curling cross delivered with pace, often from deep or wide positions. The curve makes it difficult for defenders to clear, bending into the space between the defender and the goal.

Key Principles:

  • **Outside Foot:** Using the outside of the foot for a strong, curling trajectory.
  • **Pace over Loft:** The ball must travel fast and low/medium height.
  • **Aim:** Targeting the area just behind the center back's head for a striker's run.
